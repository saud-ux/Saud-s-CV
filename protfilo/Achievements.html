Saud Achievements

Saud Alhamd has achieved the first place in lawn tennis three times and secured the second place five times. He has joined the Saudi national team twice. Saud has been playing in the Premier League with Al-Fals Zulfi Club for nine consecutive years, contributing significantly to their qualification.
